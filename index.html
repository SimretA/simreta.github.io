I am a third year Ph.D. student at the University of Notre Dame advised by Prof. Toby Li. My research focuses on Human-AI interaction. Specifically, I use cognitive learning theories to design and evaluate tools that support humans and AI collaborate in ambiguous and uncertain settings. Grounded in cognitive learning theories, my work explores how to support users in aligning their intentions, preferences and values during model training, fine-tuning, and evaluation processes.

I am greatful to be supported by the IBM Ph.D. research for the 2024/25 academic year. Prior to this I got my Bachelor of Science from Addis Ababa University in Software Engineering.
